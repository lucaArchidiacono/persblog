# Privacy Policy

smolweb does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app fetches a public web feed and displays it on your device. No accounts are required.

The app uses [TelemetryDeck](https://telemetrydeck.com) for privacy-friendly, anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal data or track individual users. For more details, see [TelemetryDeck's privacy policy](https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/).

If you have questions, reach out via the [support page](/smolweb/support/).
