# Privacy Policy

smolweb does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app fetches a public web feed and displays it on your device. No accounts are required. No analytics or tracking tools are used. No data leaves your device.

If you have questions, reach out via the [support page](/smolweb/support/).
